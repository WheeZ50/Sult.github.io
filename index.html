Something about myself

I started my Eve career not to long ago in the early spring of 2013. At start it was hard to grasp all the aspects of the game. Using the famous Internet to get more and more information about eve I decided that manufacturing was the direction I wanted to go. The idea was to be able to do everything myself from mining the ore to producing the products and everything in between. Soon I realized that that was pretty skill heavy but that didn't hold me back.

After some time I found a new corporation and got myself into Null-sec. Almost immediately I discovered that the market in Null-sec was as good as dried up. Everything got imported from Jita or other places in High-sec. So here I found my mission: "Lets get this market flowering as well."
Together with my corporation members we should be able to offer a lot of items here a swell, for a fair price.

I encountered some challenges in Null-sec. My skills in manufacturing are not yet sufficient to produce the most desired items. My market skills could use some improvement, which is why I built this site! This way I am able to manufacture based upon your demands and aim for the sharpest price the market has to offer. Since I will not be wasting my time crafting materials that end up looking pretty for window shoppers.

Please lets help each other out and take matters in our own hands. Let our territory flourish and show our neighbors what we can do.

With kind regards,
Sult Dizarra
